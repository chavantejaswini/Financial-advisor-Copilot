Advisor Meeting Prep Copilot

One AI-powered workflow to prepare for client meetings faster and more consistently

Today’s challenge
Advisors spend significant time manually reviewing fragmented information (CRM notes, portfolio activity, market updates, client goals, compliance) before each meeting. This is time-consuming, inconsistent across advisors, and makes it harder to deliver timely, personalized recommendations at scale.
Proposed solution
An AI copilot that synthesizes client context, identifies risks and opportunities, surfaces relevant market and compliance context, and suggests talking points and next-best actions—while clearly separating facts, assumptions, and recommendations and keeping the advisor in control of every decision.

What the copilot delivers in one click

  • Client summary and key financial/relationship signals
  • Potential risks and opportunities
  • Suggested discussion topics and recommended next-best actions
  • Confidence notes and areas requiring human review
Reduce prep time — Less manual gathering and reading across systems.
Improve consistency — Same structure and quality of prep for every meeting.
Better decisions — Relationships between goals, portfolio, market, and compliance surfaced clearly.
Trust & compliance — Human review built in; facts vs recommendations clearly labeled.
